Namavali game

You are presented with a grid of 9 jumbled up names of 3 Hindu gods (given as clues). Your task is to identify the 3 groups of 3 names in utmost 4 wrong attempts with the help of up to 3 hints.

Select 3 names from the grid that you think belongs to the same god and click Submit to check if your guess is correct. Find all the 3 groups without making 4 mistakes. You can get upto 3 hints revealed as coloured text.

Click the New Game button to begin
