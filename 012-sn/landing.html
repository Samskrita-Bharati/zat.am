Welcome to the Surya Namaskar Game

This interactive game helps you learn the 12 names of the Sun (Surya) in Sanskrit through a drag-and-drop puzzle!

How to Play:

  • Click on the center sun image to reveal a Sanskrit term.
  • Drag the displayed term and drop it into the correct outer circle location.
  • If you're right, the circle will be filled and the progress will update.
  • If wrong, an error count (❌) will be shown.
  • Use arrow keys (← →) to rotate the circles and view better.
  • Complete all 12 placements to win!
